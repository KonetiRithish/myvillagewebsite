About

My village

Mothalu is a small and beautiful village located in Indukurupeta Mandal, in Nellore district, Andhra Pradesh.The village is surrounded by green fields and natural beauty. Most of the people in Mothalu depend on agriculturefor their livelihood. Farmers grow crops like paddy, vegetables, and other seasonal crops.

Village Families

The families in Mothalu live together with love and unity. Most families follow traditional values and respect elders.In our village, everyone knows each other, and people share happiness and problems together. The bonding between familymembers is very strong. All villagers treat each other like one big family.

Festivals in Our Village

Festivals in Mothalu are celebrated with great joy, devotion, and unity. During Vinayaka Chavithi and Grama Devatha Sri Mahalakshmi Ammavaru festival, all families participate together with devotion and happiness. The whole village gathers for poojas, cultural programs, and traditional celebrations. These festivals strengthen the bond between villagers and bring everyone closer like one big family.

Vinayaka Chavithi

During Vinayaka Chavithi, villagers install Lord Ganesha idols and perform pooja with devotion. Children and elders participate happily. Cultural programs and prayers are conducted, and prasadam is distributed to everyone.

The villagers also celebrate the festival of Mahalakshmi Ammavaru, who is the Grama Devatha (village goddess).Special poojas and rituals are performed for the well-being and prosperity of the village. All families gather together and celebrate with devotion and unity.

Unity and Togetherness

One of the special qualities of Mothalu village is unity. Every family member participates in festivals and social activities. People help each other in times of need. Because of this strong bond, everyone in the village feels close to each other.

Conclusion

Mothalu is not just a village; it is a place filled with love, tradition, and togetherness. The unity among villagers and the joyful celebration of festivals make our village very special and peaceful.
